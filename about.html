I'm a solo creative living in Montreal, Quebec who loves interesting and beautiful things. A website needs to be both: interesting enough to make them come, and beautiful enough for them to stay a while. Inspired by visual art and design throughout history, I frequently incorporate original 3D art to help each project speak with its own distinct voice. I create my websites from scratch using source code meaning that they are fast, reliable, and customizable. It also means that I can make you a simple and easy to use backend that feels posting, not navigating tangled menus.
